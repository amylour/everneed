Got Some Questions?

A product is considered eco-friendly or sustainable when it is made from renewable resources, has minimal impact on the environment during its lifecycle, and is produced through ethical and responsible methods. This includes using organic or recycled materials, employing energy-efficient manufacturing processes, and ensuring minimal waste and pollution. Additionally, sustainable products often have a longer lifespan and are designed to be recycled or biodegraded at the end of their use.
Yes, we prioritise sustainable packaging for all our products. Our packaging materials are either recyclable, compostable, or made from recycled content. We constantly evaluate and seek new methods to reduce our packaging footprint and encourage our customers to recycle or compost the packaging after use.
We have a strict vendor assessment process in place. Our suppliers are required to meet our sustainability and ethical standards, which include fair labor practices, responsible sourcing of raw materials, and minimal environmental impact. Regular audits and site visits are conducted to ensure our suppliers adhere to these standards.
Sustainable products can sometimes be more expensive due to the higher quality, eco-friendly materials used, and the ethical and environmentally-friendly production methods employed. The processes involved in creating sustainable products often prioritise long-term environmental benefits over short-term cost savings. However, in the long run, many customers find that sustainable products offer better value due to their durability and reduced impact on the environment.
Each product we sell comes with specific disposal or recycling instructions. Most of our products are designed to be either recyclable or compostable. If you're unsure, please refer to the product label or contact our customer service for guidance. We also partner with local recycling initiatives to help our customers find convenient drop-off locations for certain products.
